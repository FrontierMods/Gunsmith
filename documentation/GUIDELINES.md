# Guidelines

## Stats

Unless stated otherwise, values add up. Three different factors worth +1 each add up to a modifier of +3.


### Pistol Grips

#### Handling

* +4 — any grip
* +1 — front side / back side to the grip
* +1 — having a texture
* +1 — the texture is rough or grooved
* +1 — full-wrap grip
* +1 — back lip
* +1 — finger grooves
* +1 — rubber parts


##### Modifications

* +1 — duct tape wrapping
* +1 — paracord wrapping
* +2 — rubber sleeve
